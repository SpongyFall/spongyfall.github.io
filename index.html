About Me

Along with a BS in Computer Science, I'm a competitive gamer who loves to learn and build projects. As a game developer for 5+ years, I have 3,000+ recorded development hours. As a hardcore gamer, I own hundreds of games and have 14,000+ hours played.

When I begin a project, I finish it. I don't like to drop things, I develop till the requirements are satisfied, then I'll add a little more. I work tirelessly to achieve completion, and won't stop till the players get an update. I take critique very well.

I've always loved to learn and improve, no matter the situation. Just ask my professors, mentors, or anyone I know. They'll tell ya.

I love survival, RPG, and competitive multiplayer games. I grew up playing them, have thousands of hours in some, and is why that mix of progression, exploration, and player-driven stories is a huge part of why I make games.
